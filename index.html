Jutro is a cultural non-profit organization in Zagreb, Croatia.

Our work focuses on arts, heritage, and human rights.

We have worked with Silvia Amancei and Bogdan Armanu, Maximilian Lehner, BLOK, Culture Hub Croatia, and the LGBT Centre Split.

Our work is supported by the City of Zagreb, Gwaertler Foundation, and Moleskine Foundation.
